About Me

I'm a second year Industrial Design student at TU Delft with a passion for combining creativity, technology and functionality. Long before university I was already deep into 3D modelling in Blender, which is ultimately what led me to study design. Over time that hobby turned into freelance work with over 200 clients, and eventually into professional roles at design agencies in Delft and Liverpool.

University Projects

A collection of design projects from my time at TU Delft. Some were completed individually, others in collaboration with a team. Each project pushed me to combine research, ideation and prototyping to solve real problems across different subjects.

FIELD - Agriculture Robot

We designed an agricultural robot concept that scares away birds from crop fields using AI detection, a camera system, and a non-damaging laser mounted on a servo-driven pole. The prototype was built with Arduino and electrical components to demonstrate the idea on a small scale.

SafeSense - Bike Safety

A collaborative university project focused on designing an autonomous robot to assist farmers with monitoring crops and collecting environmental data. My role focused on concept development, form exploration, and 3D modeling of the final design.

PainPointer.com

SaaS

A SaaS tool I built that helps entrepreneurs and founders discover business opportunities by scanning Reddit for real customer pain points. Users can select a subreddit and the tool uses AI to analyse recent posts and surface genuine frustrations that could be worth solving.
